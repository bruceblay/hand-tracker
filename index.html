hand-tracker

webcam-driven musical experiments. click "enable hand control" to navigate by pinching.

  • theremin hands
    left hand: y = pitch, x = resonance. right hand: y = volume, x = filter cutoff.
  • air drums hands
    four-piece kit. pinch to hit. hand height picks the drum, swing speed sets the volume.
  • face fx face
    a drone that responds to your face. open mouth opens the filter, brows up wets the reverb, smile distorts.
  • finger counter hands
    hold up some fingers. it counts. up to ten with both hands.
  • tetris hands
    left hand picks the column and pinches to drop. right hand twists to rotate.
  • tombola hands
    a spinning hexagon of bouncing notes. pinch to drop a ball. spin the hex with one hand. open your other hand to crack the walls.
  • emoji mirror hands · face
    your face and hands replaced with the closest matching emoji. peace sign, thumbs up, smile, surprise, kiss.
  • connect four hands
    two-player. pinch to grab a token, move your hand to pick a column, release the pinch to drop.
  • secret handshake hands
    set a sequence of hand shapes as a password. left-pinch to record each. then re-enter to unlock.
  • face drums face
    six drums triggered by facial expressions. open mouth, smile, brows up, pucker, wink left, wink right.
  • 3d shapes hands
    pick a shape, move your hand to rotate it, spread two hands apart to zoom in.
  • pond surface hands
    your reflection rippling on water. pinch either hand to drop a stone.
  • paint hands · face
    draw lines, stamp emojis, erase. right hand draws. left hand or your face picks the emoji.
  • pdf annotator hands
    load a pdf and pinch + drag to highlight passages. tap "sign" to draw a signature and place it.
